✦ Philosophy

Design Principles

The core tenets behind the neo-brutalist aesthetic — inspired by MotherDuck's design system and applied to portfolio design.

🔤

Type Does the Work

Pick a strong monospace font and let it carry the brand. Display-size typography becomes the visual signature.

▬

Borders Over Shadows

2-3px solid outlines create hierarchy better than soft drop shadows. Hard edges signal confidence.

▣

Color in Blocks

Flat saturated panels define sections — no gradients. Cream canvas + one accent color drives the page.

✦

Playfulness as Trust

A confident product can afford to be charming. Tilted CTAs, doodles, and mascots humanize technical content.

⬟

Offset Shadows

Hard -6px 6px offset shadows replace soft elevation. They give tactile affordance without fake 3D.

⊞

4px Grid System

Everything snaps to a 4px base. Consistent padding (40px, 16px, 8px) creates rhythm across sections.
